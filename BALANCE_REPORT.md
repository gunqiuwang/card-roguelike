## Balance Issues

### Problems Identified
1. **Strike too weak** - 6 damage for 1 energy = 6 DPE, but enemy HP is 30-40
2. **Heal too weak** - 5 HP for 2 energy is poor value
3. **Starting deck is all防御** - No sustain in early game

### Suggested Improvements
- Increase Strike damage to 8 (current DPE is too low)
- Increase Heal to 8 HP for 2 energy
- Consider adding 1 more starter attack card

## Win Rate Estimation
- Normal enemies: ~70% (with basic strategy)
- Elite enemies: ~40% (need better cards)
- Boss: ~30% (requires strong deck)
